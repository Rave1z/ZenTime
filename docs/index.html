Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

ZenTimeRelax ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our meditation timer mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Only

ZenTime is designed with privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

1.1 Meditation Session Data

We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Session History: Date and time of completed meditation sessions
  • Session Duration: The length of each meditation session (in minutes)
  • Ambient Sound Preference: The ambient sound selected for each session (if any)
  • Session ID: A unique identifier for each session (UUID)

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and remains on your device only. You can clear this history at any time through the app settings.

1.2 Notification Permissions

The App may request permission to send you local notifications for:

  • Meditation session completion alerts
  • Daily meditation reminders (if enabled)

Notification permissions are managed by iOS and can be changed at any time in your device settings. We do not have access to your notification preferences beyond what iOS provides.

1.3 Audio Playback

The App may request access to your device's audio system to play ambient sounds during meditation sessions. All audio files are bundled with the app and played locally.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we use the information solely for:

  • Displaying your meditation session history
  • Providing statistics about your meditation practice
  • Enhancing your user experience within the app

We do not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect personal identification information

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by ZenTime is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's UserDefaults system. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Data is protected by iOS security measures
  • If you delete the app, all associated data is also deleted
  • Data is not synced to iCloud or any cloud service

4. Third-Party Services

ZenTime does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate with social media platforms or data collection services.

The App uses only Apple-provided frameworks and services that are part of iOS, such as:

  • AVFoundation (for audio playback)
  • UserNotifications (for local notifications)
  • UserDefaults (for local data storage)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Your Data: Access your meditation session history at any time through the app
  • Delete Your Data: Clear all session history through the app settings
  • Uninstall: Delete the app to remove all associated data
  • Notification Settings: Control notification permissions through iOS Settings

6. Children's Privacy

ZenTime is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone. Since all data is stored locally and no personal information is collected, the App is safe for children to use.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
